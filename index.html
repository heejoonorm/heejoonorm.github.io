Hi, I am a M.S. student in Artificial Intelligence at Yonsei University, advised by Prof. Jongmin Lee. I am currently a member of the Data Intelligence and Learning (DIL) Lab.

My research interest lies in the mathematical foundations of Reinforcement Learning (RL). I emphasize precise mathematical modeling and seek to understand where theoretical assumptions diverge from real-world settings.

By interpreting methods through intuitions from mathematical analysis (e.g., functional analysis), I aim to design RL algorithms that are theoretically grounded yet effective in practice.
